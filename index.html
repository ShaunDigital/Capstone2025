AIME

An AI-powered mental health assistant for anxiety and panic management

View Prototype

Project Overview

AIME is designed to help users manage anxiety and panic attacks through personalized support using AI and biometric feedback. This project explores how tech can empower mental health care beyond traditional methods.

Research

Research included analysis of mental health apps like ADA, Wysa, and Youper. Interviews and feedback are planned with users and professionals to refine the app experience.

Design Process

Initial wireframes and moodboards were developed using pen & paper and Figma. The design focuses on clarity, calm visuals, and accessibility.

Features

  • Chatbot for managing panic attacks
  • Smart symptom checker
  • Wearable device integration
  • Custom treatment path suggestions
